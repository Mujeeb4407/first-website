Croele Butter Injection Marinade

This marinade is an excellent way to infuse rich, savory flavors
directly into meat, ensuring it stays tender and juicy when cooked.
It works well for poultry, pork, and even beef.

Ingredients

  • 1 stick Butter
  • 2 tablespoon of cajun seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion Butter

Instructions

  1. Melt the Butter in a small bowl
  2. Add cajun seasoning and garlic powder, whisking until well combined
  3. Fill an injector with the mixture and inject it into your meat at the different angle
  4. Roast or cook as desired
